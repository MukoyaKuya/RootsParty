Our Preamble

The Roots Party of Kenya is a revolutionary political movement committed to restoring dignity, justice, and prosperity for every Kenyan. Our mission is clear: empower the people and reclaim the nation.

We believe in harnessing our own resources, revitalizing our economy, and reclaiming our sovereignty. We reject foreign dependency, corruption, and inequality.

Vision

A prosperous, united, democratic society with empowered citizens under servant leadership.

Mission

Lead Kenya’s transformation through patriotism, social justice, gender parity, youth empowerment, and global repositioning.
